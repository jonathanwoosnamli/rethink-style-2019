Act for mental health today - sign our petition

Please take a moment to sign the petition below calling on the Government and politicians to reform the Mental Health Act. 

"The Mental Health Act is badly out of date. It fails to respect the rights and autonomy of people treated under it, or how they want their loved ones involved in their care.  

We are really pleased to hear that the Prime Minister has welcomed the final report by the Independent Review of the Mental Health Act and that the Government plans to bring forward a new mental health bill.

We now call on the Government and politicians from all parties to accept the recommendations of the Independent Review in full and to make delivering reform of the Mental Health Act a priority."

Together we can make a difference!
