Light Poetry Book

Light is a book composed of poems from members of Rethink Mental Illness.

From sonnets to acrostics this book features poems of all kinds depicting the theme of light and for many how this relates to their mental illness.

These poems are sure to stick with you.

All of the proceeds from this book supports the work of Rethink Mental Illness.
